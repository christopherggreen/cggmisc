Christopher G. Green's Miscellaneous Functions

A variety of functions written by Christopher G. Green (@christopherggreen). Notable functions include qqmathFitted, which produces quantile-quantile (Q-Q) plots against a fitted distribution. The package also includes utility routines for fitting Student's t distribution; their main use is in constructing Q-Q plots against a Student's t distribution.
